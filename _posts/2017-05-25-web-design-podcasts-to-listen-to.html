I write code for the most part of every day. Sometimes I listen to music but on occasion a Podcast on a topic that surrounds Web design. It helps me keep up to date with what's going on in the industry. Here are a few of my favourite Web design podcasts.

1. ShopTalk

A Web Design and Development Podcast with Chris Coyier and Dave Rupert.

2. Front End Happy Hour

A podcast featuring a panel of Software Engineers from Netflix, Evernote, Atlassian & LinkedIn talking over drinks about all things Front End development.

3. User Experience Podcast - UXpod

A platform for discussing topics of interest to practitioners or others with an interest in UX.

4. Motion And Meaning

A podcast about motion for digital designers brought to you by Val Head and Cennydd Bowles.

5. Developer Tea

Developer Tea is a podcast for web and software developers hosted by a developer that you can listen to in less than 10 minutes.
